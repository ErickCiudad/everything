How to Play




▲           Attack      W

◄           Parry         A

▼           Block         S

►   Power Attack   D

Shift       Back Away     Q

Regular attacks use 1 stamina, and deal 1 damage.

Play          

Power attacks use 3 stamina, and deal 3 damage.

If a hit connects and is blocked, the damage is dealt to stamina instead

if an attack is blocked while you have no stamina, you become stunned for 2 seconds

Getting hit while stunned deals one extra damage

If you parry someone right as they would strike you, you stun them.

Lastly, don't hold down your keys. Most computers have trouble with that.
